ABOUT ME

XR Creator and Immersive Art Researcher

VR design professor

I believe in the sanctum of the body, where sinews and circuitry intertwine, a symbiotic ballet unfolds, an intricate choreography of flesh and technology. Embracing the digital tempest, I dance with the binary tides, nurturing a symbiotic bond that transcends mere transaction. Within this convergence, where the immaterial blurs with the bodily-felt and the imaginary with the strangely real, I carve a path through the digital expanse, weaving a narrative of empathy and consciousness. Here, pixels pulse with the heartbeat of intention, and algorithms hum in harmony with the whispers of our humanity.

Yesica Duarte


In the realm of my artistic practice, I delve into the intimacy where flesh meets the digital extension of VR, a symbiotic narrative unfolds that transcends the mere limits of perception. My exploration revolves around the immersive fabric of XR and wearable interfaces, using biodetection to sense the breath. At the liminal threshold between the tangible and the digital, I attempt to navigate the knowledge of the subtle body, unraveling the unique space in which the human experience of intuition is sculpted. Adopting the role of performer in the realm of XR installations, I create a safe space for an intimate interaction between the user and the performer. It is the invitation that I extend towards a sensory journey, trying to unravel the language of immersive technology through performance. In the midst of this narrative, the intention is to employ the breath as an interface for deep listening, resonating with the essence of a digital dreamscape..

My main interests lies in:

>Development & design for VR

>Biosensing and Wearable Interfaces

Hybrid Live Performance
